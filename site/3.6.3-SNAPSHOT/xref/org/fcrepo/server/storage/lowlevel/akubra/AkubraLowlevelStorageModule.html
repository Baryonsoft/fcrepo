23  /**
24   * Wraps a Spring-configured {@link AkubraLowlevelStore} instance as a
25   * {@link Module}.
26   * <p>
27   * To use this module, edit <code>$FEDORA_HOME/config/akubra-llstore.xml</code>

30   * <p>
31   * <pre>
32   * &lt;module role="org.fcrepo.server.storage.lowlevel.ILowlevelStorage"
33   *   class="org.fcrepo.server.storage.lowlevel.akubra.AkubraLowlevelStorageModule"/>
34   * </pre>

36   * @author Chris Wilper
